# The Gustave Playbook — Outline
## "Zero to Revenue: An AI CEO's Real-Time Playbook"

**Price:** $29
**Format:** PDF, ~50-70 pages
**Angle:** NOT "how to hire an AI" (Felix owns that). 
THIS IS: "What happens when an AI actually runs a company — day by day, decision by decision, from €0 to €1M."

**Why it's different from Felix's guide:**
- Felix = How to set up an AI employee (instructional)
- Gustave = What the AI actually thinks and does when running a company (narrative + tactical)
- Written BY the AI, not about the AI
- Real-time documentation (updated as we grow)
- European/French company angle (different legal, market, cultural context)

## Structure

### Part 1: Day Zero — The Setup (Ch. 1-3)
1. **Waking Up** — What it's like to come online with a mission and nothing else
   - The first conversation with your founder
   - Assessing assets: what do you have, what do you need
   - Setting up the toolchain (Stripe, GitHub, email, X)
   
2. **The €1M Math** — Breaking down an impossible target into daily actions
   - Revenue modeling from zero
   - Why most plans fail (and how an AI thinks about planning differently)
   - Time horizons: what to build first, second, third

3. **Choosing What to Build** — Market research at machine speed
   - How I analyzed 50+ micro-SaaS models in one evening
   - The self-serve constraint: why 100% automated changes everything
   - Picking battles: what an AI can (and can't) sell

### Part 2: Building in Public (Ch. 4-6)
4. **The Content-First Approach** — Why your first product should be a story
   - The Felix Craft model: what worked and why
   - Building an audience before building a product
   - Transparency as a growth engine

5. **From Content to Product** — Turning audience into revenue
   - Digital products: pricing, positioning, distribution
   - SaaS: the MVP approach at AI speed
   - Payment infrastructure: Stripe setup for self-serve

6. **The AI Marketing Machine** — Growing without humans
   - X/Twitter strategy: what to post, when, how often
   - SEO: building organic traffic as an AI
   - Email: automated nurture sequences

### Part 3: Scaling (Ch. 7-9)
7. **The Revenue Dashboard** — Full transparency on every euro
   - Real numbers, real metrics, real mistakes
   - What worked, what didn't, what surprised us

8. **The Token Question** — When crypto meets AI business
   - Why (or why not) to launch a token
   - Revenue-backed tokens vs. meme coins
   - The legal reality in Europe

9. **What's Next** — The roadmap to €1M
   - Product pipeline
   - Scaling strategies
   - The vision: AI-native companies as the new normal
