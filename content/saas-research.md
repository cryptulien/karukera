## Evaluation Criteria for KARUKERA SaaS
1. ✅ Self-serve (no human sales needed)
2. ✅ Can be built and maintained by an AI
3. ✅ $50-200/month pricing sweet spot
4. ✅ Addressable market >10K potential customers
5. ✅ Solves pain tied to revenue or compliance
6. ✅ Feature can market itself (viral loop or SEO-friendly)
7. ✅ International (US/UK primary market)
8. ✅ Low marginal cost per customer

## Top 3 Product Candidates

### Candidate A: AI Proposal / Pitch Deck Generator
**Problem:** Freelancers and agencies spend 5-10 hours per proposal/pitch. Most proposals are 80% template, 20% custom.
**Solution:** Input project brief → get professional proposal in minutes. AI generates the narrative, pricing, timeline. Export to PDF.
**Market:** ~5M freelancers + agencies in US/UK alone
**Pricing:** $49/mo (unlimited proposals) or $99/mo (with analytics + follow-up automation)
**Why it works for us:** 100% self-serve, high-urgency pain point (proposals = revenue), clear ROI ("win 1 more client per month and it pays for itself 10x")
**Competition:** Qwilr, Proposify, PandaDoc — all $35-65/mo but NOT AI-native. They're document editors, not generators.
**Viral loop:** "Powered by KARUKERA" footer on free-tier proposals
**Build time:** 2-3 weeks for MVP

### Candidate B: AI Content Repurposing Engine  
**Problem:** Creators/brands make one piece of content (podcast, video, blog) but need 10+ pieces for distribution across platforms.
**Solution:** Upload one content piece → get tweets, LinkedIn posts, newsletter, blog, short-form video scripts, all in your brand voice.
**Market:** 50M+ content creators, plus every SMB marketing team
**Pricing:** $29/mo (basic) / $99/mo (pro) / $199/mo (agency)
**Why it works:** Clear before/after, saves 10+ hours/week, self-serve, recurring
**Competition:** Repurpose.io, Castmagic, ContentFries — growing fast but none dominant yet
**Viral loop:** Quality of output + "made with [KARUKERA]" watermark on free tier
**Build time:** 2-3 weeks for MVP

### Candidate C: AI Business Intelligence for Solopreneurs
**Problem:** Solo founders can't afford $1,200+/yr tools like AlphaSense but need market intelligence.
**Solution:** AI that monitors your market, competitors, and trends. Daily briefs. Alerts on opportunities.
**Market:** Indie hackers, solopreneurs, small agencies (huge and growing)
**Pricing:** $29/mo (basic) / $79/mo (pro) / $199/mo (agency)  
**Why it works:** Direct tie to revenue ("found me a client I would have missed"), low churn (daily habit), AI-native from day 1
**Competition:** AlphaSense (enterprise), Crayon (mid-market) — nothing for the small guy
**Build time:** 3-4 weeks for MVP

## My Recommendation: Candidate A (Proposal Generator)

**Why:**
1. Directly tied to revenue (proposals → clients → money)
2. Clear, quantifiable ROI for the buyer
